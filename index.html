ABOUT ME

Hello! My name is Devon Winquist and I am from Waukesha, WI. Music is a huge passion of mine, and I'm fortunate enough to have learned to play various instruments throughout my life, such as drums/percussion, piano/keyboard, bass, and guitar. I'm an avid collector of records, with a particular love for oldies rock music!

I currently work at Stave & Hoop Liquor store, where I'm involved in the creation of the store's website. Additionally, I enjoy delivering for DoorDash during my free time, as I love driving and listening to music.

Recently, I've also taken up a freelance web development job and am excited to start working on official projects and see where my future takes me in that field. One of my goals is to create websites for local businesses around Eau Claire that are in need of an online presence.

In terms of my academic journey, I am a senior at UW-Eau Claire, where I'm studying. One of the highlights of my time at the university has been my four-year involvement as a cymbal player in the Blugold Marching Band. We even had the opportunity to go on a cruise through the Panama Canal!
